# Privacy Policy for Epic Sudoku

Effective date: 5 June 2026  
Last updated: 7 June 2026

Warren Row Games ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Epic Sudoku mobile game for Android.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Epic Sudoku may process, how that information is used, and the choices available to users and parents or guardians.

Epic Sudoku is designed to collect as little personal information as possible. Epic Sudoku does not currently include third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

## 1. Information We Collect

Epic Sudoku may process limited information depending on the features used.

### a. Gameplay and app data

Epic Sudoku may store gameplay-related information locally on the user's device, including:

- Current and completed puzzles
- Game progress
- Completion times
- Statistics
- Achievements or achievement progress
- Settings and preferences
- Saved and in-progress puzzles
- Puzzle library data

This gameplay and app data is stored locally on the user's device and is not sent to a Warren Row Games server.

### b. In-app purchase data

Epic Sudoku offers optional in-app purchases, such as Puzzle Packs, through Google Play Billing.

When a purchase is made:

- The transaction is processed by Google Play
- Payment information is handled by Google
- Warren Row Games does not collect or store payment card details
- Epic Sudoku may receive purchase confirmation information from Google Play so that purchased content can be unlocked in the app

Google's use of billing-related information is governed by Google's own privacy policies and account settings.

### c. Puzzle import and text recognition data

Epic Sudoku may include an optional puzzle import feature that uses Google ML Kit for text recognition.

Where this feature is used:

- The feature is only used when the user chooses to import a puzzle
- Image or puzzle data is processed for the purpose of recognising puzzle content
- Text recognition is intended to run on the user's device
- Warren Row Games does not operate a separate server for storing imported puzzle images

### d. Device and technical data

Epic Sudoku and the Google Play or Android platform services used with the app may process limited technical data, such as:

- Device type
- Operating system version
- App version
- Crash or diagnostic information made available through platform tools
- Information needed to maintain app functionality, security, billing, and compatibility

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information processed by Epic Sudoku to:

- Provide gameplay features
- Save progress, puzzles, statistics, and preferences
- Support optional in-app purchases
- Unlock purchased puzzle content
- Support optional puzzle import features
- Maintain app functionality, security, and stability
- Diagnose and fix bugs
- Respond to privacy or support requests

## 3. Data Storage

### Local storage

Epic Sudoku stores gameplay-related information locally on the user's device, including:

- Preferences
- Statistics
- Current and completed puzzles
- Saved and in-progress puzzles
- Puzzle library data
- Achievement or progress-related data

This local data remains on the user's device unless it is deleted by the user, the app's storage is cleared, or the app is uninstalled.

### Cloud or third-party platform storage

Epic Sudoku does not currently operate its own dedicated external user account system or independent cloud backend.

If Google Play Billing or other Google Play services are used, certain information may be processed by Google through Google Play or Android platform services.

## 4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information.

Epic Sudoku does not currently include third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

We may share or allow processing of limited data only where necessary to provide app functionality through third-party platform services, such as:

- Google Play Billing
- Google Play / Android platform services
- Google ML Kit, where the optional puzzle import feature is used

These providers may process information under their own terms and privacy policies.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Epic Sudoku may be played by children, but Epic Sudoku is not designed to knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Epic Sudoku does not currently include third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, chat, messaging, public profiles, or a Warren Row Games-operated user account system.

Some platform features, such as Google Play Billing, Google Play services, Android permissions, and device-level controls, may be managed by Google and may depend on the user's Google account, device settings, parental controls, and Google Play settings.

Parents or guardians can help manage a child's experience by:

- Reviewing device and Google Play account settings
- Managing purchase approvals and parental controls through Google Play
- Managing notification permissions through Android settings
- Clearing app data through Android device settings
- Uninstalling the app
- Contacting us with privacy-related questions

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information to us inappropriately, please contact us at privacy@warrenrow.com and we will review the request.

## 6. Permissions Used

Epic Sudoku may request or use the following permissions or device capabilities, depending on the features used and the Android version:

### Internet access

Used for Google Play Billing and related Google Play services. Epic Sudoku does not use internet access to operate a Warren Row Games user account system or independent analytics service.

### Network state

Used to check whether network connectivity is available for billing or platform-service operations.

### Notifications

Used only if the user enables optional reminders, such as daily challenge reminders. Notification permissions can be managed through Android device settings.

### Storage, photo, or file access

Used only where the user chooses to import or export puzzle files or puzzle images. Access depends on the Android version and the file or photo picker features available on the device.

## 7. Your Choices

Users, parents, or guardians may:

- Use Epic Sudoku without creating a Warren Row Games account
- Choose whether or not to make optional in-app purchases
- Manage Google Play purchase settings through Google
- Enable or disable notifications through Android device settings
- Import or export puzzles only when choosing to use those features
- Clear app data through Android device settings
- Uninstall the app at any time
- Contact us with privacy-related questions

Please note that clearing app data or uninstalling the app may delete locally stored game progress, statistics, saved puzzles, preferences, and puzzle library data.

## 8. Data Deletion

Epic Sudoku does not currently operate its own user account database.

Locally stored Epic Sudoku data can be deleted by clearing the app's storage through Android device settings or by uninstalling the app.

If Google Play Billing or other Google Play services are used, some purchase or platform-related information may be managed by Google. Relevant Google account, Google Play, and purchase settings can be managed through Google.

Privacy-related questions or deletion requests can also be sent to:

privacy@warrenrow.com

## 9. Data Retention

We retain no direct server-side user account database for Epic Sudoku at this time.

Locally stored data remains on the user's device until it is removed, app storage is cleared, or the app is uninstalled.

Where Google Play Billing, Google Play services, or Android platform services are used, retention may be governed by Google's systems and policies.

## 10. Security

We take reasonable steps to keep Epic Sudoku secure and to minimise unnecessary data collection. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Epic Sudoku stores gameplay and puzzle data locally on the user's device and does not currently operate a separate Warren Row Games server for user accounts, analytics profiles, or gameplay history.

If Epic Sudoku introduces advertising, analytics, cloud accounts, online profiles, community features, or other data-connected features in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe the related data handling and control options.
